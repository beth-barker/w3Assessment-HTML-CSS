Sugar House

Sugar House is a family-friendly neighborhood in Salt Lake City, UT that has many restaurants, breweries, and parks. Here are some recommendations:

Restaurants

  • The Dodo

    • The Dodo is great for brunch and is famous for their desserts.

  • Nomad East

    • Nomad East has great pizza and shareables.

  • Wasatch Brew Pub

    • Wasatch Brew Pub has great beers, cocktails, and burgers.

Parks

Sugar House Park

Sugar House Park has a big walking path with huge fields in the middle. It has a pond. It's dog friendly(on-leash) and great for sledding during the winter time.

Fairmont Park

Fairmount park is the perfect place to be active. It has tennis and pickleball courts as well as a walking trail. There is a dog park connected and the rest of the park is dog-friendly.
